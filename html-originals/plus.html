Questions
Common questions.
Is Property Pros still free for Property Owners without Plus? +
Yes — absolutely. Property Pros is and always will be free for Property Owners. Posting projects, receiving quotes, messaging contractors, and hiring pros costs nothing on the free plan. Plus is an optional upgrade for Property Owners who want additional benefits like contractor discounts, Charity contribution option, and priority support.
How do the contractor discounts work? +
Participating contractors opt into the Plus discount program and offer member-only deals — typically 10–30% off their services. When you're a Plus member, those deals are visible in your dashboard and in your monthly email. The discounts are negotiated directly with contractors, so they're real savings, not inflated-then-discounted prices.
How does the charity contribution work? +
During checkout, you can choose a participating charity or nonprofit to receive 50% of your monthly membership fee. If you pay $0.99/month, $0.50 goes to your chosen organization automatically every month. You can change your charity anytime from your account settings. Property Pros manages the disbursement — you don't need to do anything after the initial selection.
Can I cancel Plus anytime? +
Yes — cancel from your account settings anytime. No cancellation fees, no questions asked. If you cancel mid-month you retain Plus access until the end of your billing period. Your account reverts to the free plan automatically.
Why $9.99/year instead of monthly billing? +
One payment, zero hassle. $9.99 covers you for a full year — that's less than $1 a month. It keeps things simple and means you're never hit with a charge you forgot about. If you use even one contractor discount during the year, the membership pays for itself many times over.
