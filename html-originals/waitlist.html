Questions
Common questions.
When exactly are you launching? +
We're targeting a public launch in Rhode Island in late summer 2026. Waitlist members will get early access before the public launch — contractors first, then homeowners and realtors. We'll email you with exact dates as they're confirmed.
Is the waitlist free? +
Completely free. There's no credit card, no commitment, and no obligation. Homeowners will always be free on Property Pros. Contractors and realtors get a free trial when we launch.
What perks do waitlist members get? +
Early access before the public launch, priority placement in search results for contractors in the first 90 days, and founding member status. We may add additional perks as we get closer to launch — waitlist members hear about those first.
I'm a contractor — how is this different from Thumbtack or Angi? +
Three things: First, $9.99/month flat versus paying $15–$100 per lead regardless of whether you win the job. Second, you get a full business suite — leads, quotes, schedule, earnings, messaging — not just a lead list. Third, we have a two-way rating system with homeowner Trust Scores, so you can see who you're quoting before you respond.
I'm a realtor — what makes this worth $29.99/month? +
The inspection tracker alone is worth it — it keeps deals on track by helping you manage repair requests before closing deadlines. Add the client management tools, referral system, and the fact that referring 10 active clients makes your subscription free — and it pays for itself quickly.
Will you expand beyond Rhode Island? +
Yes — Massachusetts is next, then Connecticut and New Hampshire. We're launching in Rhode Island first to get it right in one market before scaling. If you're outside Rhode Island, join the waitlist anyway and we'll notify you when we reach your area.
