Questions
Frequently asked
Can I post projects on behalf of my clients?+
Yes — this is one of the core features of the Realtor Pro account. You can post projects using any client's property address, managed from your own dashboard. Your clients don't need to be on Property Pros themselves, though you can also invite them to create their own free homeowner account linked to your referral.
Do my clients need to pay anything?+
Never. Homeowners on Property Pros are always free. Your clients can create a free account, receive quotes, message pros, and manage their own projects at no cost. You pay the $29.99/month Realtor Pro subscription, and they get access to the platform as a benefit of working with you.
What's the difference between a Realtor account and a homeowner account?+
A homeowner account is free and lets you manage your own single property. A Realtor Pro account at $29.99/month gives you a full client management dashboard, the ability to manage projects for multiple clients and properties, post-inspection repair tracking, pre-listing checklists, closing gift referral links, a preferred contractor list, and a public realtor profile. It's built specifically for the workflow of a working real estate professional.
How does the closing gift referral link work?+
At or after closing, you generate a unique referral link for that client from your dashboard. The link is pre-personalized with your name, photo, and a welcome message you write. When the client clicks it and creates a free homeowner account, they're automatically linked to you as their referring realtor. Every time they post a project or get a quote, you can see it in your client activity feed.
Can I cancel if I don't find it useful?+
Absolutely. Cancel anytime from your billing settings — no forms, no phone calls, no penalty. Your 14-day free trial gives you full access with no credit card required. And if you complete 30 days of paid subscription and don't find it valuable, contact us and we'll refund your first month under our money-back guarantee.
Does Property Pros help with listings or home sales?+
No — Property Pros is a home services marketplace, not a real estate listing platform. We don't handle MLS listings, home valuations, buyer-seller matching, or any transactional real estate. Our focus is entirely on connecting homeowners and realtors with verified local contractors for repairs, renovations, and maintenance.
