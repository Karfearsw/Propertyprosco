Why we do this

Built in Rhode Island.
Giving back to Rhode Island.

Property Pros started in Rhode Island because we believe local communities deserve local tools. The contractors on our platform live here. The homeowners are our neighbors. It only made sense that the business we're building gives something back to the place that's supporting it.

We didn't want the charitable component to be an afterthought or a marketing line. So we built it into the price from day one — half of every Plus™ subscription goes to nonprofits working in RI communities. Not someday. From the first signup.

FAQ
Common questions
Does every homeowner contribute, or just Plus™ subscribers?
Only Plus™ subscribers ($9.99/year) contribute to the fund. The basic Property Pros experience is free for homeowners and always will be — the charitable component is part of the Plus™ value proposition, not a requirement.
What does "referral code" mean for nonprofits?
Each nonprofit partner receives a unique referral code (e.g. HABITAT-RI). When a homeowner signs up for Plus™ using that code, the $4.99 from their subscription is allocated directly to that organization. The nonprofit promotes the code to their community — donors, volunteers, newsletter subscribers — and every signup through their code directly benefits them.
What happens to subscriptions with no referral code?
Subscriptions without a referral code contribute to the general Property Pros Community Fund. These funds are distributed to active partner organizations. Partners who drive more referrals receive proportionally more from the general pool.
How do nonprofits receive their donations?
Partner organizations receive donations via direct bank transfer. Distribution details, frequency, and reporting are agreed upon at the time of partnership. We provide partners with a dashboard showing referral activity and allocation amounts.
Is my contribution tax deductible?
The $9.99 Plus™ subscription is a payment for services (contractor discounts, platform features, AI tools) and is not tax deductible as a charitable donation. The $4.99 portion is allocated to the nonprofit partners by Property Pros as part of our business model. We recommend consulting a tax professional for your specific situation.
Can I see how much has been donated in total?
We'll publish an annual impact report showing total donations distributed to partner organizations. We won't display a real-time balance — we believe the mission speaks for itself without needing a live counter.
