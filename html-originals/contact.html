FAQ
Frequently asked questions
Filter by topic or search above to find what you need.
How do I post a project as a homeowner? +
Sign up for a free homeowner account, then click the "Post a project" button from your dashboard. Describe the service you need, your location, budget range, and ideal timeline. Verified pros in your area will send quotes, usually within a few hours. There is no cost to post — ever.
How do I choose the right pro for my project? +
Compare pros based on their ratings, number of reviews, verified badges, portfolio photos, and response time. You can message any pro before hiring to ask questions or request a free estimate. We recommend reading at least 3–5 recent reviews and confirming the pro is licensed and insured for your type of project.
Is Property Pros really free for homeowners? +
Yes — completely free. Homeowners can create an account, post unlimited projects, message pros, and receive quotes at no cost. There are no hidden fees, no credit card required, and no commissions taken from your project. Pros pay a monthly subscription to access leads, which keeps the platform free for homeowners.
How do leads work for service pros? +
When a homeowner posts a project that matches your services and location, it appears in your Leads & Quotes dashboard. You can view the full project details, message the homeowner, and send a custom quote — all from within the platform. Responding quickly significantly increases your chance of winning the job.
How do I get the verified pro badge? +
Go to Profile Settings and navigate to the Portfolio tab. Upload your business license and insurance certificate. Our team reviews all documents within 24 hours and grants the Verified badge once approved. The badge appears on your profile, in search results, and on all your quote cards — building trust with homeowners immediately.
How many leads can I expect per month? +
This varies based on your service type, location, and how active your profile is. On average, active pros in competitive markets receive 10–20 matched leads per month. Completing your profile with photos, a bio, and credentials significantly increases your visibility.
When does my billing start? +
All pro plans include a 14-day free trial. Your card is not charged until the trial ends. After that, you are billed monthly on the same date. You can cancel at any time before the trial ends and you will never be charged. If you cancel after being billed, your plan remains active until the end of that billing period.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan? +
Yes, you can change your plan at any time from the Billing page. Upgrades take effect immediately and you are only charged the prorated difference. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. There are no contracts or penalties for changing plans.
What is your refund policy? +
We offer a full refund within 7 days of your first charge if you are unsatisfied for any reason. After 7 days, refunds are evaluated on a case-by-case basis. To request a refund, contact our support team at billing@propertypros.com with your account details.
How do I reset my password? +
On the login page, click "Forgot password?" and enter your email address. You will receive a reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if you don't see it. The link expires after 24 hours — if it expires, simply request a new one.
How do I delete my account? +
For pro accounts, go to Profile Settings → Security and scroll to the Danger Zone section. For homeowner accounts, contact our support team and we will process your deletion within 5 business days. Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone — all your data, messages, and history will be removed.
Can I have both a homeowner and a pro account? +
Yes — you can maintain separate homeowner and pro accounts using different email addresses. We do not support dual-role accounts under a single email at this time. If you need help managing both, contact our support team and we can help set it up cleanly.
What is the Realtor Pro account and who is it for? +
Realtor Pro is a $29.99/month account type built specifically for licensed real estate professionals. It gives you a full client management dashboard, the ability to post projects on behalf of clients, a post-inspection repair tracker, pre-listing project checklists, personalized closing gift referral links, and a preferred contractor shortlist. It's designed to help realtors manage contractor relationships for clients at every stage of a transaction — without touching listings or real estate sales.
Can I post projects on behalf of my clients as a realtor? +
Yes — this is one of the core features of Realtor Pro. From your dashboard, select a client from your roster and post a project using their property address. The project goes live under their account and they receive notifications when quotes arrive. You can monitor all activity from your realtor dashboard without your clients needing to do anything themselves.
How do referral links work for realtors? +
From your Referrals page, you can generate a unique, personalized referral link for each client at closing. The link includes your name, photo, and a custom welcome message. When a client signs up through your link, they're automatically connected to you and you can see their project activity in your dashboard. You also earn $3/month in credits for every active referred client — which can offset your monthly subscription.
Do my clients need to pay to use Property Pros? +
Never. Homeowners — including your clients — always use Property Pros completely free. There's no subscription, no fees, and no commissions. You pay the $29.99/month Realtor Pro subscription, and your clients get full access to the platform as a benefit of working with you.
