Questions
Everything you need to know.
Is Property Pros really free for homeowners? +
Yes — completely and permanently free. There is no subscription, no premium tier, no commission on jobs won, and no hidden fees of any kind. Homeowners will never pay a cent to use Property Pros. Contractors pay a flat $9.99 per month which is how we keep it free for you.
How fast will I get quotes? +
Most homeowners receive their first quote within 2 hours of posting. For popular trades like roofing, painting, and driveways, you'll often have 3 or more quotes within the same day. Emergency projects can sometimes be quoted within 30 minutes.
How are contractors verified? +
Every contractor on Property Pros goes through a manual verification process before they can respond to any leads. We confirm their contractor's license with the state licensing board, verify their general liability insurance certificate, and validate their identity. The Verified badge only appears after all three checks pass.
Am I obligated to hire anyone who quotes me? +
Never. You can receive as many quotes as you want, message any pro, and walk away without hiring anyone — all at no cost. You only hire if and when you find a pro you're completely comfortable with.
What if the work turns out badly? +
After every completed job, both parties leave reviews. If you had a bad experience with a contractor, leave an honest review — it helps protect other homeowners and creates accountability on the platform. For serious disputes, contact our support team and we'll help mediate.
What trades are covered? +
Property Pros covers all major home improvement trades including roofing, driveways and paving, masonry, painting (interior and exterior), landscaping, electrical, plumbing, HVAC, junk removal, general remodeling, bathroom and kitchen renovation, flooring, fencing, and more. If it's a home project, we have pros who can do it.
