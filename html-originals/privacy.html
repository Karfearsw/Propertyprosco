Section 01
Overview & Who We Are

Property Pros, LLC ("Property Pros," "we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information about you when you use our website, mobile applications, and related services (collectively, the "Platform").

Property Pros operates as a marketplace platform connecting homeowners with independent service professionals across the United States. We are the data controller for personal information processed through the Platform.

✅
Our core promise: We will never sell your personal information to third parties, use it for purposes you haven't consented to, or share it in ways that aren't described in this policy. Your trust is the foundation of our business.

This policy applies to all users of the Platform, including homeowners, service professionals, and visitors who have not created an account. By using the Platform, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

Section 03
How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Operating the Platform — creating and managing your account, matching homeowners with service pros, processing quotes, enabling messaging, and providing all Platform features
  • Payment processing — billing Service Pro subscriptions, processing refunds, and detecting fraudulent transactions
  • Safety and trust — verifying pro credentials, calculating Trust Scores, moderating content, and investigating reports of misconduct
  • Communication — sending transactional emails (account confirmations, quote notifications, billing alerts), and service-related notifications
  • Marketing — sending promotional emails and newsletters, only if you have opted in. You can opt out at any time.
  • Platform improvement — analyzing usage patterns to improve features, fix bugs, and optimize performance
  • Legal compliance — meeting our obligations under applicable law, responding to lawful requests from authorities
  • Dispute resolution — reviewing message history and account activity when investigating reported violations or disputes
ℹ️
We rely on the following legal bases for processing your data: contract performance (operating your account and delivering the service), legitimate interests (improving the Platform, preventing fraud), legal obligation (tax and regulatory compliance), and consent (marketing emails, optional analytics). You may withdraw consent at any time.
Section 04
How We Share Your Information

We never sell your personal data. We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

Between homeowners and service pros: When a Service Pro submits a quote on a homeowner's project, limited homeowner profile information (first name, display name, neighborhood, and Trust Score) becomes visible to that pro. Full contact details (phone number and email) are only shared if the homeowner has enabled contact sharing in their settings. Project addresses are never publicly displayed.

Service providers: We work with trusted third-party providers to operate the Platform. These providers are bound by data processing agreements and may only use your data to perform services on our behalf:

Legal requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, court order, or government authority, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of Property Pros, our users, or the public.

Business transfers: If Property Pros is acquired by or merged with another company, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you by email before your information is subject to a different privacy policy.

🚫
We do not share your personal information with advertisers, data brokers, marketing companies, or any third party for their own commercial purposes. Property Pros products are ad-free and we do not allow third-party advertising targeting on our platform.
Section 05
Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to operate the Platform, remember your preferences, and understand how you use our service. You can manage cookie preferences in your Account Settings under "My Data."

Cookie typePurposeRequired?
Essential Session management, login authentication, security tokens, CSRF protection Required
Functional Remembering your preferences, saved filters, display settings, and notification choices Optional
Analytics Understanding how users navigate the Platform so we can improve the experience (via Google Analytics with IP anonymization) Optional
Performance Monitoring page load times and error rates to keep the Platform fast and reliable Optional

Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they are necessary for the Platform to function. You can opt out of all other cookie categories in your account settings or by using your browser's privacy controls. Note that disabling functional cookies may affect your experience.

⚠️
We do not use advertising cookies or allow third-party ad networks to set cookies on our Platform. No behavioral advertising or cross-site tracking is conducted through Property Pros.

Section 07
Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information. To exercise any of these rights, visit your Account Settings or contact us at privacy@propertypros.com. We will respond within 30 days.

📋
Right to access
Request a copy of all personal information we hold about you, including account data, project history, messages, and ratings. Available as a downloadable export from your Data settings.
✏️
Right to correction
Request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete personal information. Most information can be updated directly in your account settings without contacting us.
🗑️
Right to deletion
Request that we delete your personal information. You can delete your account from Settings → Security → Danger zone. Some data may be retained as required by law (e.g., billing records).
📦
Right to portability
Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format (CSV or JSON) so you can transfer it to another service. Available via Settings → My Data → Request export.
🛑
Right to object
Object to processing of your personal information for marketing purposes at any time. Unsubscribe links are included in all marketing emails. You can also manage preferences in Notification settings.
⏸️
Right to restriction
Request that we limit how we use your data in certain circumstances — for example, while we are verifying the accuracy of data you have disputed. Contact privacy@propertypros.com to request restriction.
🔒
To protect your privacy, we may verify your identity before processing certain requests. We will never charge a fee for exercising your rights unless a request is clearly unfounded, repetitive, or excessive — in which case we will explain our reasoning before charging any fee.
Section 08
Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect it from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Our security practices include:

  • Encryption in transit — all data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher (HTTPS)
  • Encryption at rest — sensitive data stored in our databases is encrypted using AES-256
  • Password hashing — passwords are never stored in plain text; we use bcrypt with a strong salt
  • Payment security — all payment data is handled by Stripe, which is PCI DSS Level 1 certified. We never store full card numbers.
  • Access controls — employee access to production systems is restricted by role, requires multi-factor authentication, and is logged
  • Regular audits — we conduct periodic security reviews and vulnerability assessments

Despite these measures, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at security@propertypros.com.

⚠️
Data breach notification: In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you by email within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, and will provide information about what happened, what data was affected, and steps we are taking to address it.
Section 09
Children's Privacy

Property Pros is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@propertypros.com and we will promptly delete that information from our systems.

If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 18 without verifiable parental consent, we will take immediate steps to remove that information and close the associated account.
