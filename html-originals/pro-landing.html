Questions
Answers for contractors.
Is it really just $9.99 with no per-lead fees? +
Yes. $9.99 per month, flat. No per-lead fees, no commission on jobs won, no premium tier with more leads. Every pro pays the same amount and gets access to every single feature and lead on the platform. That's it.
How many leads will I get? +
It depends on your trade, service area, and how active you are. Pros with complete profiles (bio, photos, verified badge, service area set) receive significantly more leads than those without. Most active pros in competitive trades receive 10–25 matched leads per month. You can also actively browse and quote projects beyond your matched leads.
What trades are covered? +
All major home improvement trades — roofing, driveways and paving, masonry, painting, landscaping, electrical, plumbing, HVAC, junk removal, general remodeling, bathroom and kitchen renovation, flooring, fencing, and more. If homeowners need it done, we have a category for it.
What is the Verified badge and do I need it? +
The Verified badge appears on your profile after we've confirmed your contractor's license and insurance certificate. You don't need it to use the platform but verified pros consistently win more jobs — homeowners filter for verified pros and trust them significantly more than unverified ones. Getting verified takes about 5 minutes and is well worth it.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes — cancel anytime from your billing settings. No cancellation fees, no questions asked. If you cancel mid-month you'll retain access until the end of your billing period. If you're in your 14-day trial, you pay nothing.
How is this different from Thumbtack or Angie's? +
Three fundamental differences. First, pricing — we charge $9.99/month flat, they charge $15–$100 per lead. Second, accountability — our two-way rating system and homeowner Trust Score creates mutual accountability that doesn't exist on other platforms. Third, tools — we give you a full business suite (schedule, earnings, messaging, job management) not just a lead list.
