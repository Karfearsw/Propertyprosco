Questions
Frequently asked
Is it really just $9.99 — no hidden fees?+
Yes. $9.99 per month, billed monthly, and that's it. No setup fees, no per-lead charges, no premium add-ons, no commissions on jobs you win. Everything on the platform is included in that single price.
Do I need a credit card to start the free trial?+
No credit card required to start your 14-day free trial. You'll add payment details before your trial ends, but you won't be charged until then. Cancel before day 14 and you'll never pay a cent.
What happens after the 14-day trial?+
After 14 days, your subscription starts at $9.99/month. You'll receive a reminder email before the trial ends so you're never surprised. If you cancel before day 14, nothing is charged — ever.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Yes — cancel anytime from your billing dashboard in one click. No cancellation fees, no forms, no phone calls. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period.
How does the 30-day money-back guarantee work?+
Complete your profile, respond to at least 5 leads in your first 30 days, and don't win a single job — contact our support team and we'll refund your first month in full. No questions asked. We're that confident.
Is the price going to go up later?+
We will always give at least 60 days' notice before any price change, and existing subscribers will be grandfathered at their current rate for a full year before any increase applies. We're committed to being fair.
Do homeowners pay anything?+
Never. Homeowners use Property Pros completely free — no subscription, no fees, no commissions. Service pros pay $9.99/month and realtors pay $24.99/year, which is how we keep it free for homeowners and sustainable for everyone.
What's included in the Realtor Pro plan?+
The $24.99/year Realtor Pro plan includes a full client management dashboard, the ability to post projects on behalf of clients, a post-inspection repair tracker, pre-listing project checklists, personalized closing gift referral links, a preferred contractor shortlist, a public realtor profile, and a referral credit system that pays you $3/month per active client you refer. Everything is included — no add-ons.
