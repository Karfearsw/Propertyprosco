Ratings & Reviews

Two-way rating system. Property Pros operates a two-way rating system in which Owners rate Service Pros and Service Pros rate Property Owners after a job is completed. Both parties agree to provide honest, good-faith ratings based on their actual experience.

Rating anonymity. Ratings are kept anonymous until both parties have submitted their rating for a given job, after which both ratings become visible. This is designed to encourage honest feedback without fear of retaliation.

Prohibited rating conduct. The following rating behaviors are prohibited and may result in account suspension:

  • Submitting ratings for jobs you did not perform or participate in
  • Rating a counterparty before a job has been completed or agreed upon
  • Coordinating with others to artificially inflate or deflate ratings
  • Threatening to leave a negative review unless certain demands are met
  • Leaving reviews that contain false factual claims

Rating disputes. If you believe a rating is false, defamatory, or otherwise violates these Terms, you may flag it for review through your account or by contacting support@propertypros.com. We will investigate and take appropriate action, which may include removing the rating. We do not remove ratings solely because a party disagrees with the content.

Trust Score. Property Owners are assigned a Trust Score (0–100) based on their payment history, communication ratings, project history, and other factors. This score is visible to Service Pros and is designed to help them make informed decisions about which projects to quote.
