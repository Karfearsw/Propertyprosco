Property Owners FAQ
Is it really free for Property Owners?
Yes — completely free. Posting a project, receiving quotes, messaging pros, and leaving reviews all cost nothing. We charge contractors a flat $9.99/month subscription, so we never need to charge owners or sell your contact info to lead services.
How quickly will I get quotes?
Most projects receive their first quote within 2 hours during business hours. Popular trades like junk removal and painting typically respond even faster. You'll receive a notification when a new quote arrives.
How do I know the pro is legitimate?
Every pro on Property Pros has a verified identity. Pros in licensed trades (roofing, electrical, plumbing, HVAC, general contracting) must upload their state license and insurance certificate, which we manually review. Other trades are phone and email verified.
What if I'm not happy with the work?
We have a dispute resolution process to help mediate. We strongly recommend keeping all communication on the platform and getting the scope of work in writing before the job starts. See our Dispute Resolution page for details.

Contractor FAQ
How is this different from Thumbtack or HomeAdvisor?
Those platforms charge $25–$80 per lead whether you win the job or not. We charge a flat $9.99/month and you can quote unlimited projects. If you send 5 quotes a month and win one job, you've already paid for the subscription many times over.
How many leads will I get?
It depends on your trade and service area. During our Rhode Island launch, you'll be one of the first verified pros on the platform — which means less competition for every lead posted. Early subscribers typically see the best results.
What does verification involve?
For licensed trades (roofing, electrical, plumbing, HVAC, general contracting, structural masonry): upload your state license number and a current certificate of insurance. We review and approve within 1–2 business days. For all other trades, just confirm your email and phone number — no documents required.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel from your billing settings with one click. No cancellation fees, no phone calls required. Your profile stays visible through the end of your billing period.

Realtor FAQ
How much does it cost?
$24.99/year, billed monthly. Includes full client management, unlimited project postings, priority matching, and access to your referral dashboard. 14-day free trial — no credit card required.
Can I share access with my team?
Team accounts are on our Phase 2 roadmap. For now, each realtor has their own account. If your team wants early access to multi-seat pricing, contact us at support@propertypros.com.
How does the referral program work?
Share your unique referral link with clients, other realtors, or contractors. When they sign up and subscribe, you earn credits toward your monthly fee. Refer enough and your subscription pays for itself.
