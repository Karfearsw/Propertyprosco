How it started

We got burned by a bad contractor. So we built something better.

In 2022, our founder hired a contractor for a simple roof repair. The job took three times longer than quoted, cost double the estimate, and the contractor vanished before finishing. Sound familiar?

He wasn't alone. Every homeowner has a story like this. The endless phone tag. The vague estimates. The no-shows. The regret of hiring someone you knew nothing about.

Today, Property Pros connects homeowners with verified, rated, and insured local professionals across Rhode Island — with a two-way rating system that keeps everyone honest.

What we stand for

Our values

Everything we build, every decision we make, comes back to these principles.

Accountability on both sides
We built a two-way rating system so both homeowners and pros are accountable to each other. Good behavior is rewarded. Bad behavior is visible. Everyone wins when both sides show up honestly.
Verification you can trust
Every verified pro on our platform has submitted their license and insurance for review. We don't hand out badges — we earn them through a real document review process before any pro gets the checkmark.
Free for homeowners, always
Homeowners should never pay to find help for their home. Our platform is 100% free for homeowners — no hidden fees, no commissions, no credit card required. Pros pay a subscription that funds the platform.
⚡
Speed matters
We know home repairs can't wait. That's why we built our platform to connect homeowners with pros fast — average response time under 2 hours, with same-day availability indicators on every pro card.
Truly local, everywhere
We're not just a national directory. Every market we enter gets verified pros with real reviews and local track records. Whether you're in a major metro or a small town, we find you someone real — not just someone available.
Pros deserve to grow
Small contractors and local tradespeople are the backbone of our communities. We give them the tools to compete, grow their reputation, and win more business without the complexity of running their own marketing.
How Property Pros works

Simple for everyone involved in the home.

1
Homeowner posts a project
Takes 2 minutes. Describe the job, set a budget range, and pick a timeline. No account required to browse — sign up only when you're ready to post.
2
Verified pros send quotes
Matched pros in your area receive the lead and can send a detailed quote with pricing, timeline, and a personal message — usually within hours.
3
Compare and choose with confidence
Review ratings, portfolios, verified credentials, and quotes side by side. Message any pro directly before committing — no pressure, no obligation.
4
Both sides rate each other
After the job, homeowners rate pros and pros rate homeowners. This two-way system creates a fair, honest marketplace where reputation matters for everyone.

Why pros switch to Property Pros
You know exactly what you'll spend every month
No surprise bills at the end of the month. No $300 charge because you checked your leads on a busy Saturday. One flat monthly rate — whether you respond to 5 leads or 50. Your marketing budget is finally predictable.
You can rate problem customers — not just be rated by them
On every other platform, you're at the mercy of the homeowner. Here, you can rate them too. Customers who don't pay, change scope, or disrespect your crew get flagged. Good customers stand out. Everyone is accountable.
Your whole business in one place — not just leads
Quote tracker. Job calendar. Messaging. Billing. Reviews. Other platforms drop you a lead and leave. We give you the tools to close it, schedule it, complete it, and get reviewed — all without leaving the platform.
Your verified badge actually means something
We don't hand out verified badges like stickers. We review your actual license and insurance documents before you earn the checkmark. That badge tells homeowners something real — not just that you paid for a membership.
Leads from homeowners who are actually ready to hire
We require homeowners to describe their project in detail before it goes live. Vague postings don't make it through. That means the leads you receive are from people who are ready — not browsing, not price-testing, not wasting your time.
No contracts. No annual commitments. No risk.
Try it free for 14 days. No credit card required upfront. Cancel before the trial ends and you'll never see a charge. We'd rather earn your business month by month than lock you in. That's the difference.
