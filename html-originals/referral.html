Common questions
When do credits hit my account? +
Credits are applied within 24 hours after your referral completes the qualifying action — for homeowners, that's posting their first project; for contractors, completing the free trial.
Is there a limit to how many friends I can refer? +
No limit at all. Refer as many people as you want. Every qualifying referral earns you credits, and milestone bonuses stack as you hit them.
What can I use credits for? +
Credits apply toward Plus™ membership. Future uses — including discounts from local contractors — are coming in Phase 2. Credits never expire as long as your account is active.
What does my referral get? +
Homeowners who sign up through your link get $5 off their first Plus membership. Contractors get an extra 7 days added to their free trial (21 days total). Realtors get one month free on their first subscription.
